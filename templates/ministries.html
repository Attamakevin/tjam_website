Perpetual Adoration

Our core ministry focuses on continuous worship and adoration of the Blessed Sacrament. We maintain 24/7 adoration with dedicated adorers taking shifts to ensure Jesus is never alone.

  • 24/7 Eucharistic Adoration
  • Holy Hour sessions
  • First Friday devotions
  • Adorer recruitment and training
